## 👨‍💻 About Me

I'm a **Frontend Developer** from Gorakhpur, India with **3+ years of hands-on experience** building modern, responsive, and high-performance web applications — across freelance projects, product companies, and agency environments.

My work sits at the intersection of **design and engineering**. I don't just write code — I think about how things *look*, *feel*, and *behave* on every screen size and device. I take Figma files and turn them into pixel-perfect, accessible, and animated interfaces that users actually enjoy using.

Over the years I've worked with early-stage startups, insurance companies, and AI product teams — collaborating with designers, backend engineers, and product managers to ship real products on real deadlines.
